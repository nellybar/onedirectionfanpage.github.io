How did One Direction form?

The British-Irish boy band formed in 2010 on the British television show The X Factor. Nicole Scherzinger and Simon Cowell came up with the idea to combine the solo artists into a group.


When did One Direction go on hiatus?

In 2016, One Direction announced an indefinite hiatus after Zayn Malik left the band.


Who is the oldest member of One Direction?

Louis Tomlinson is the oldest member, born on December 24, 1991. Harry Styles is the youngest member, born on February 1, 1994.


How many records have One Direction sold?

As of 2020, One Direction has sold 70 million records worldwide, making them one of the best-selling boy bands of all time.


What awards has One Direction won?

One Direction has won seven Brit Awards, seven American Music Awards, six Billboard Music Awards, five Billboard Touring Awards, and four MTV Video Music Awards.


Who is the richest member of One Direction?

Harry Styles is the richest member of One Direction with a net worth of US\$120 million.
