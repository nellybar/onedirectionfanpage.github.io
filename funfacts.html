Most Watched Music Video

It should come as no surprise that the most watched music video is for "What Makes You Beautiful," One Direction's all-time best-selling single. After placing third on The X Factor in 2010, the song, which served as their debut single, rocketed to the top of the UK charts and remained there for four weeks. With 4.8 million copies sold, it was certified quadruple Platinum in the US in June 2016.

It sold one million copies and received three platinum certifications in the UK. Over 1.4 billion people have seen the music video on YouTube to date.
